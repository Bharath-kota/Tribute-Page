Facts about Lesser Lord Kusanali

  • She was created by her predecessor, Greater Lord Rukkhadevata by planting the purest branch of irminsul that was not corrupted by the forbidden knowledge
  • She was then imprisoned in Sanctuary of Surasthana by the sages of Sumeru
  • Later a group of people, along with a certain traveler named Lumine save her from her imprisonment after 500 years
  • She then proceeds to punish the sages accordingly while keeping true to her gentle naure, only exiling the sages to a certain part of the nation to atone for their sins
  • She then fulfills the mission of her predicessor by erasing her existence from irminsul to erase the forbidden knowledge from the world to prevent further catastrophes
